Development

Finally, I spent the rest of the 3 months to bring the designs to life!
Project management throughout the lifecycle was handled through Trello. I met with the Senior Mobile Developer and Product Manager daily for standups. In these standups, we tracked our progress, assigned tasks and their story points, and updated our backlog. I integrated APIs and Flutter widgets to develop the first MVP of the application. Screens and features I worked on include the screens for the Login/Registration flows, the Profile page, and a Reset Password feature.
