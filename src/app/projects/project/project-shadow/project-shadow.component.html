High Fidelity Prototypes

Splash/Registration/Login Pages

Our goal was to develop an accessible screen design while maintaining the standard sign-up/login flow. We accomplished this by focusing on labeling buttons for their respective functions and labeling inside text fields.

Deposit Money Pages

We prioritized confirmation messaging and avoided deceptive patterns to provide a seamless, simplified process for users to deposit money.

Development

Finally, I spent the rest of the 3 months to bring the designs to life! Project management throughout the lifecycle was handled through Trello. I met with the Senior Mobile Developer and Product Manager daily for stand-ups. In these stand-ups, we tracked our progress, assigned tasks and their story points, and updated our backlog. I integrated APIs and Flutter widgets to develop the first MVP of the application. Screens and features I worked on include the screens for the Login/Registration flows, the Profile page, and a Reset Password feature.
