Animated Typography: Telfar

Role: Computer Graphics Designer
Type of Project: Academic
Library: p5.js

Problem Statement: Create a landing page for a brand's website, focusing on animated (and optionally interactive) typography. Think about stylistic choices in the context of a client-driven project and their brand (color, typefaces used, overall feel,etc.) but are also encouraged to creatively/wildly experiment.

Challenge: To create animated typography for a brand/person/org's website. Can be a fixed proportion, but should fill the screen's width automatically.

What I did: I used p5.js to create an animated landing page for Telfar.

Design Brief

Telfar is genderless fashion label based in Bushwick, Brooklyn. The queer Black-owned brand was founded by Telfar Clemens in 2005. The Telfar Shopping Bag is the biggest staple of the brand, made of vegan leather and branded with the signature "T" logo.
Highlighted in the design brief are:

  • Audience: words to describe the target audience of Telfar
  • Visual & Motion Words: descriptors used to inspire the typography
  • Font Samples
  • Color Schemes
  • Mood Board: specific design inspirations

My main inspiration was sourced from Hong Kong PolyU Design Shows in 2013. I loved how the typography overlaps each other to create a dynamic image with simple geometry. I knew I could code something as dynamic and make it come to life.
