Mercedes Santana Fashion Website

Role: Web Developer
Type of Project: Freelance
Software: Angular, Firebase

Objective: To redesign a website for a fashion designer.

Timeline: May 2023 - August 2023

Background

Mercedes Santana is a Haute Couture fashion designer with more than 30 years of experience. I conducted an interview to gather her business and customer needs, wants, and pain points. With this information, I redesigned and developed a new website that best reflected her brand identity and values. I implemented responsive design principles to ensure a seamless user experience across various screen sizes.
